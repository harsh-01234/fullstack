A Weekend in Jaipur: Royal Palaces & Pink Streets

Waking Up in the Palace

tart your morning early with a trip to Amer Fort (Amber Fort), located about 11 km from Jaipur city. This 16th-century fort is perched on a hill and built from red sandstone and marble. Inside, you'll find stunning mirror work in the Sheesh Mahal and sweeping views of Maota Lake. Take a jeep ride or walk up the fort for the best experience.

Evening – Jal Mahal & Light Show

End your trip with a visit to Jal Mahal, the Water Palace, which appears to float on Man Sagar Lake. While you can’t enter the palace, the view is beautiful at sunset. If you have time, catch the light and sound show at Amer Fort in the evening to complete your royal experience.
